Citrus-Pineapple Smoothie Bowl

Description

This smoothie bowl is a fun way to switch up your routine. It features vitamin Câ€“rich citrus fruit, heart-healthy cashews, and gut-healthy Greek yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup fat-free Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup frozen pineapple chunks
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 navel orange, segmented
  • 1/2 ruby grapefruit

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients into a blender.Blend until the mixture is smooth, and divide between 2 bowls.
  2. Top with more orange and grapefruit, plus chia seeds, unsweetened coconut flakes, and chopped cashews.

Nutrition: 240 cal, 12 g pro, 31 g carb, 5 g fiber, 19 g sugars ( 0 g added sugars), 8 g fat ( 4 g sat fat)
