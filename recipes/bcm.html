Berry, Chai, Mint Smoothie

Description

With this quick and easy recipe, youâ€™ll eat a half-cup of beets without even realizing it.

Ingredients

  • 1 c. sliced strawberries
  • 1/2 c. raspberries
  • 1/2 c. grated beet (from 1 medium beet)
  • 1/3 c. mint leaves
  • 1 tbsp. chia seeds
  • 1 c. unsweetened almond milk

Steps

  1. Place berries, beet, mint, and chia seeds in resealable plastic bag or freezer-safe jar. Freeze overnight or longer.
  2. When ready to prepare, add almond milk to blender, then add frozen ingredients. Blend until smooth. Serve in two tall glasses.

Nutrition per serving: 105 cal, 3 g pro, 17 g carb, 8 g fiber, 7 g sugars (0 g added sugars), 3.5 g fat (0.5 g sat fat), 0 mg chol, 115 mg sodium
